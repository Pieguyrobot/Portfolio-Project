Christmas Tree Shops

I got my first job at the Christmas Tree Shops in Greenwood around the end of September of 2021. I have been working this job for almost 9 months now as of July and hace learned a lot from this job. My role is Customer Experience Sales, this just means I'm a "floor worker". As a floor worker we do tasks such as stocking, cleaning, organzing, helping customers and even time to time hop on the registers to try and get the line down. This job has taught me valuable soft skills such as communication, working with a team and perserverance. Juggling this with all 4 of my AP classes last year I felt made me more of a timely organized person as I had to plan out my day and homework schedule around these 25-30 hrs a week.
